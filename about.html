About The Amaltaas Fort

At The Amaltaas Fort, we envision a place where every event is a unique experience, surrounded by the charm of a beautiful fort and the serenity of water. The Rajwada-style mandap reflects the heritage of Rajasthan, while the amphitheater captures the essence of romance and festivity. Our lush green lawns bring you closer to nature making every event come to life.

Exciting times lie ahead as we embark on our expansion journey. Soon, we will offer 8-10 meticulously designed rooms and two dormitories, ensuring your guests experience comfort and luxury. Additionally, a restaurant will be at your service, providing culinary delights that match the grandeur of our venue.

At The Amaltaas Fort, we're not just a venue; we're a destination where memories are crafted, and celebrations become timeless. Join us on this enchanting journey, where every detail is designed to create moments that last a lifetime.
